Three generations of experience.

Our company all started with one man and his passion. Delton Brown Sr. passed his passion on to his children, grandchildren, and generations to come. He was a school trained clock maker and was accredited as a CW21 by the American Watch and Clock Institute (AWCI). He started our company in 1978. Brown’s Family Clock Shop is just what it says it is- a family clock shop. Delton Brown Jr. (second generation) has carried on the family business with over 40 years of experience. He is joined by his youngest daughter, Sarah Dosch (third generation). We highly value customer service and honest repair and servicing. We know and understand how special and valuable your clock is to you and we want to bring it back to life for you.
