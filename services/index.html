Repair and service

We repair and service all makes and models of clocks, including:

  • Mechanical clocks (Grandfather, cuckoo, mantel, wall, anniversary, and antique clocks)
  • Clocks with animation and music (Melodies in Motion and Rhythm clocks)
  • Those with battery movements (Chime and melody alarm clocks)

We are all inclusive and can help you with whatever you need. Our company and services are highly recommended by Seiko.

Restoration

We offer many restoration services to fit your needs, from complete rebuilds to cabinet/casing restoration. We will bring your beloved family heirloom back to life and have it looking and running as if it was brand new!

Melodies in Motion

Seiko Melodies in Motion are one of our specialties. We are one of two repair companies that repair these special timepieces. We have an understanding of how they work and the best way to repair them, so they can live on for years to come.

Nationwide Service

Not in our area? No worries! The majority of our customers send their clocks to us to repair and service. We will treat your clock with the utmost care and respect and will package it to the best of our ability to ensure your clock returns home in better condition than we received it.
